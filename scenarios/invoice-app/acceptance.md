# Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] A user can create an invoice with client name, invoice number, due date, and at least one line item.
- [ ] The app calculates subtotal, tax or adjustment, and total without rounding surprises.
- [ ] The app separates draft, sent, paid, and overdue invoices in a way that is easy to scan.
- [ ] The app includes empty, loading or saving, validation, and error states where the workflow needs them.
- [ ] The app can export or copy a public-safe invoice summary.
- [ ] The final build includes a short explanation of what would be needed before handling real payments or tax records.
